3 Reasons to Choose OneHomes

  • 1Specialized in homestays across Japan

    Stay and engage with a local host to make the most of your time in Japan. OneHomes introduces host families that are willing to welcome you into their home as a new family member.

  • 2100% legal and approved private accommodation

    Each and every one of the host families listed on OneHomes is registered as a “Private Lodging Business Operator” certified by their local governments.

  • 3Contribution to Local Communities

    2% of the room charge will be donated for disaster relief in Japan, creating involvement between guests and the local communities. *Applicable when the total room charge (excluding service charges and tax) exceeds 10,000 yen
